

                         Maslow's Hierarchy of Jobs
                                                                   
                   +-----------------------------------+         
                   |            INTERESTING            |         
                   |      Is the work interesting?     |         
                +-----------------------------------------+      
                |                  USEFUL                 |      
                |           Is the work useful?           |                  
             +-----------------------------------------------+               
             |                   CONNECTED                   |               
             |        Is the pay related to the work?        |               
          +------------------------------------------------------+           
          |                      STABILITY                       |           
          |          Will the pay (and work) continue?           |           
       +------------------------------------------------------------+        
       |                            PAY                             |        
       |                      Will I get paid?                      |        
    +-------------------------------------------------- ---------------+     
    |                               WORK                               |        
    |                          Is there work?                          |        
   +------------------------------------------------------------------------+   
   |                               MORAL                                    |   
   |                    Is the base activity moral?                         |   
   +------------------------------------------------------------------------+
